Privacy

This website does not use cookies and does not collect personal data. We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-first measurement tool that records aggregate page views without cookies, fingerprinting, or tracking individual visitors.

If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address is held and processed by Substack under Substack's privacy policy. If you purchase a report, payment and delivery are handled by Gumroad under Gumroad's privacy policy; we receive your email address to provide support and licence administration, and nothing else.

To have your data removed from our records, email eudcmon@gmail.com.
